About Me

Welcome to Liquid Legacy! I'm Priyanshu Kumar, a Computer Science student at Lovely Professional University, committed to making a difference by raising awareness about water scarcity and conservation. With this project, my goal is to highlight the critical importance of water resources, share valuable insights, and inspire action toward sustainable water use.

Liquid Legacy was created as a platform to share knowledge, data, and stories about water availability worldwide. By exploring the challenges faced by regions near and far, I hope to inspire individuals to make conscious, informed decisions that contribute to a future where water is accessible to all.

Thank you for joining me in this journey to protect our most essential resource. Together, we can leave a legacy of water stewardship for generations to come.
